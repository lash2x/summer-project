The effects of lack of sleep

Missing sleep once or twice wont have any serious lasting effect on your health, but if you continueously lose sleep you will be at risk of serious health problems.A chronic sleep-restricted state can cause fatigue, daytime sleepiness, clumsiness and increased appetite leading to weight gain. Lack of sleep has been proven to be strongly connected to behavior as well as increasing your risk of certain illness.

    Lack of sleep can put you at risk of:
  • Slowed brain function
  • Depression
  • Stress
  • Stoke
  • Diabetes
  • Heart disease
  • Heart attack
  • Heart failure
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • High blood pressure
